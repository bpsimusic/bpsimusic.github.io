Manilla was a bill and account management app on iOS, Android and the Web. One of the big things I worked on with them was fine-tuning the First Time User Experience to incorporate the search email feature. We learned through data that new users who had five or more accounts tracked by the app had significantly better retention rates, so we engaged in this product sprint in an effort to increase account sign-up.

I worked with Product and Engineering teammates to roll out the release, mapped out the design flow and created the search screen mockups in Photoshop.
