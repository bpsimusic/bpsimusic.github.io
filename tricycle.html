Tricycle Labs built a social learning service that paired People ahead in the learning process with those behind for 1-on-1 help. The goal was to serve adults too busy for bootcamps, yet still wanted to learn with the encouragement and help of someone else. The service had a constantly updating core curriculum enabling people to help each other even if they don't teach professionally.

I worked with user researchers, designers and engineers on all aspects of the service from wirframes, full-color mockups to promotional posters.
