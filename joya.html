A friendly and easy to use app for mobile video sharing, Joya made sending and managing videos to grandparents and extended family a snap on iOS and Android. Design goals for my work with Joya focused on user acquisition, and making sure the first time user experience supported new users through sign up.

My work was informed by insights from Founder Vlada Bortnick and user research, and ranged from designing wireframes, full color email flows to compiling a visual guide based on the design language built by Vlada and the visual designers.
